**Q1. What is the primary purpose of testing according to the ISTQB Foundation syllabus?**

A) To prove that the software has no defects
B) To evaluate software quality and reduce the risk of software failure
C) To ensure the development team follows the process
D) To automate repetitive tasks

**Answer: B**
**Explanation:** Testing evaluates quality and reduces risk — it cannot prove the absence of defects.

**Q2. Which of the following is NOT a principle of testing?**

A) Testing shows the presence of defects
B) Exhaustive testing is possible for small systems
C) Early testing saves time and money
D) Defects cluster together

**Answer: B**
**Explanation:** Exhaustive testing is impossible except for trivial cases — this is one of the seven testing principles.
